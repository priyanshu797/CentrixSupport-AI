About CentrixSupport

Smart File-Based AI Support for Everyone

What is CentrixSupport?

CentrixSupport is an intelligent assistant platform designed to help users understand and query documents, files, and text content using cutting-edge AI. Whether you're a student, professional, or researcher — you can upload a file and chat with our AI to extract insights instantly.

Key Features

  • File-based Q&A support (PDFs, text files, etc.)
  • Intelligent chat assistant for contextual queries
  • Fast and responsive UI with chat history
  • No data stored – your files stay private
  • Built for simplicity, speed, and privacy

Our Mission

We believe in making AI accessible to everyone. Our mission is to empower individuals and teams to make sense of their information faster — without complex setups, steep learning curves, or security risks.
